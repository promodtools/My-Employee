About The App

My Employee Management System is a comprehensive, offline-first mobile application designed to simplify HR tasks for small business owners and managers. Whether you are running a retail store, managing a construction crew, or running a small office, our app takes the headache out of tracking attendance, calculating payroll, and storing employee records.

Built with privacy and speed in mind, all your core data lives directly on your device. You don't need a constant internet connection to manage your workforce.
